Speaker - Dexter Thomas

With much humor and laughter, Dexter will take you on an incredible journey of learning to live a life of personal power and success. He has been doing motivational speaking around the world for the past 15 years, speaking in hundreds of events at businesses, churches, universities, and prisons, to over 20,000 individuals in 10 countries. His heart is to give practical tools for improving every area of our life.

Dexter knows about motivation and overcoming because he has overcome significant difficulties in his own life. Born blind into a poor family of 8 children in a low-income community, his early life had many limitations. His insights, which come from years of experience, will help you. And his love for his audience will make you feel right at home. From side splitting laughter to deep personal insights he guarantees to make this a weekend to remember.

The Six Motivational Principles for Success

Power Talk

Facts and the stories we make up about them. Understand how words have the power to create your thinking and shape your life.

Positive Relationship

Without knowing it, we do things that hinder our relationships. Learn how to identify and change your unnoticed habits that disrupt connection. See how poor expectations affect oneness.

Health

You’re not truly wealthy until you are healthy. Have you wondered why, with all the information about health, fitness, and nutrition that is available to us, we still don't change our unhealthy habits? Learn eight health laws and how healthy habits can not only be easy to acquire, but even fun and lasting.

Change

Procrastination is a dream killer. You don’t have forever to live out your calling. Discover a stress-free, positive urgency to pursuing your dreams. Learn how to embrace change and even plan for it.

Goals to Greatness

We are our best when we are reaching, climbing, growing, aspiring, and pursuing excellence. Learn how exciting goal setting can be and how to keep the goals you make. Learn how to aspire to and practice the habits of greatness.

Money

Wrong attitudes about wealth can cripple us. Learn how blockage and flow impact wealth. Learn how to use financial stress as motivation. Your financial crisis can become a valuable tool to stimulate your creative juices. Learn how to dispel the notion that it takes money to make money.
